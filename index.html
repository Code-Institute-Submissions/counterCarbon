Take That First
Step

Measure, understand and offset your CO2 footprint

counterCarbon is a project driven by a wider concern. Much like the majority of people we were aware of "global warming", but never acknowledged the oncoming reality of such a change. This project was established with that majority in mind. As we began to acknowledge the Climate Crisis, we were conscious that many were too. counterCarbon is our effort at a first step, a straight-forward series of tools to measure and offset a person's carbon footprint.

First steps still seem to be the most difficult.
